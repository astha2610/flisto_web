Simple Steps to Your Food Memory

From your first bill scan to sharing a list with friends, Flisto keeps the flow simple. Capture once, organize into collections, understand your habits with analytics, and never lose a great meal.

01

Capture Your Visit

Snap a bill photo, pick from your gallery, or add a place manually. AI extracts line items from Indian restaurant receipts—then you review before saving.

02

Rate & Organize

Mark dishes as Loved, Okay, or Disliked. Add visits, photos, and notes—then sort places into Favorites, Want to Visit, or custom emoji collections.

03

Track Over Time

Log multiple visits per restaurant with spend totals, visit history, and dish galleries. Search and filter your library by city, recency, or distance.

04

Discover & Share

Explore analytics across spend, food taste, and places. Share a restaurant or whole collection with friends—or send a link so they can save your pick to Want to Visit.

Your food memories, visits, and collections—all in one app.
