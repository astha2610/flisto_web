Key Features

Smart Bill Scanning

AI-powered OCR extracts restaurant details, line items, and prices from bill photos—tuned for Indian restaurant receipts. Review and edit before you save.

Add Your Way

Scan a bill, pick from your gallery, or enter manually. Google Places autocomplete and map pin help you place every venue. Smart matching suggests merging with an existing restaurant.

Visit & Dish Memory

Log multiple visits with spend totals and bill photos. Rate each dish Loved, Okay, or Disliked, attach dish photos, and browse what you have eaten at every place.

Collections

Favorites and Want to Visit lists are ready on day one. Create custom collections with emoji covers, add places from home or detail screens, and share entire lists with friends.

Home Library

Search restaurants, dishes, and memories. Sort by recent visit, name, most visits, spend, or distance. Filter by city or near me when location is available.

Analytics Dashboard

Four insight tabs—Overview, Spend, Food, and Places—with filters by month or year. Track ₹ spending, liked dishes, cuisines, and how often you explore new spots.

Share Restaurants & Lists

Send a restaurant or collection to a friend in the app, or share a link anyone can open. Recipients can save your pick straight to Want to Visit—even before they have visited.

Friends & Sign In

Find friends, send requests, and build your food circle. Sign in with email, Google, or Apple—your memories and photos sync securely across devices.
