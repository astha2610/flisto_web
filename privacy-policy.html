This Privacy Policy describes how Flisto ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address, phone number, and authentication credentials.
  • Restaurant Data: You may provide information about restaurants you visit, including names, addresses, and food items.
  • Bill Images: You may upload photos of restaurant bills for processing.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Camera Data: We access your device's camera to scan restaurant bills. Images are processed to extract text and data, then stored securely.
  • Location Data: We collect your location information to show restaurant locations on maps and help you discover nearby restaurants.
  • Device Information: We may collect device type, operating system version, and app usage statistics.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Process and extract data from bill images using AI technology
  • Display restaurant locations on maps
  • Authenticate your account and maintain your profile
  • Send you important updates about the App
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

3.1 Firebase (Google)

  • Authentication: Firebase Authentication handles user login and account management.
  • Firestore Database: Your restaurant data and app preferences are stored in Firebase Firestore.
  • Storage: Bill images are stored in Firebase Storage.
  • For more information, see Firebase Privacy Policy.

3.2 Google Maps

  • We use Google Maps to display restaurant locations and provide location-based services.
  • For more information, see Google Privacy Policy.

3.3 OpenAI

  • We use OpenAI's Vision API to extract text and data from bill images.
  • Bill images are sent to OpenAI for processing and are not stored by OpenAI.
  • For more information, see OpenAI Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored securely using Firebase, which employs industry-standard security measures.
  • We use encryption to protect your data in transit and at rest.
  • We implement access controls to ensure only authorized users can access their data.
  • However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information through the App
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Opt out of location tracking (though this may limit app functionality)
  • Delete your account and associated data

6. Camera and Location Permissions

  • Camera: We request camera permission to scan restaurant bills. You can deny this permission, but you will not be able to use the bill scanning feature.
  • Location: We request location permission to show restaurant locations on maps. You can deny this permission, but location-based features will be limited.
  • You can change these permissions at any time in your device settings.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal information within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

8. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
